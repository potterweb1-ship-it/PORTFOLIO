Rural Revival

A research based plan to bring the pieces of a working downtown back to Waterloo, Indiana.
The project uses one vacant lot to add places to shop, live, work, and gather, helping bring more daily activity back to Main Street.
Role
Research · Programming · Design
Scope
Mixed-use block · ~30,470 sf
Tools
Rhino 8 · Twinmotion · InDesign
01Problem

As businesses moved toward the highway, downtown Waterloo lost much of the retail, housing, and community space that helped keep it active. Local spending leaves the county, and more remote workers have few places nearby to work or meet.

02Solution
The design brings a food hall, bodega, live work spaces, gym, public plaza, and second floor apartments together on one block. It fits into the surrounding two story brick buildings while adding more activity, housing, and shared space to the center of town.
