Andes

A custom NASCAR style paint scheme for the Andes #6 NextGen Toyota Camry, made for the iRacing sim racing series.

The design takes the Andes Crème de Menthe candy wrapper look, including the mint green mountains and script logo, and puts it onto a 200 mph race car.

Role
Livery Design & Branding
Scope
Full-Body Paint Scheme · Promo Graphics
Tools
Illustrator · Photoshop · InDesign
01Problem
Take the familiar Andes Crème de Menthe look, including the mint green mountains and script logo, and make it work on a 200 mph NextGen Camry without losing what makes the candy wrapper recognizable. 
02Solution
The paint scheme is built around the layered mountain graphic and deep Andes green. Thin horizontal contour lines pull from the wrapper texture and help give the car a sense of speed. The script logo runs across the doors, while a large cream #6 on each side makes sure the car reads clearly from every angle.
