Graham Cheese Factory Marketplace

Located in Elnora, Indiana, this project turns an abandoned cheese factory into a flexible marketplace for local businesses and the community.
The design gives the building a new purpose while creating a place for vendors, events, and everyday activity as part of Elnora’s revitalization.
Role
Design · Modeling
Scope
Adaptive Reuse · Public Marketplace
Tools
Rhino 8 · Twinmotion
01Problem
An abandoned cheese factory at a rural crossroads in Elnora needed a new use that could support local entrepreneurs and give people a place to gather.
02Solution
The design keeps the existing industrial shell and brings in natural light from above. Freestanding vendor stalls fill the main floor, while a mezzanine with long shared tables creates space for eating, working, and community events. A corrugated metal and glass exterior, along with a covered porch, gives the building a stronger presence at the edge of town.
