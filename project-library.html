Public Library East

Set on a sloped site at a major intersection in East Bloomington, this library was designed to be a community anchor for learning, sustainability, and everyday use.
The project combines a traditional library with a bike hub for repair and recycling, connecting to Bloomington’s strong cycling culture and focus on sustainable transportation.
Role
Design · Research
Scope
Public Library + Bike Hub
Tools
Rhino 8 · Twinmotion · Illustrator
01Problem
A sloped corner site in East Bloomington needed a civic building that could work with the grade while also reflecting the city’s biking culture and commitment to sustainable transportation.
02Solution

The building is organized around a long hempcrete form that splits into two wings that follow the slope. One side holds the library, while the other includes bike repair and recycling spaces. A shared entrance connects both programs, and the building extends over the slope on angled columns. A glass reading room faces the lawn and gives the library a more open connection to the site.
