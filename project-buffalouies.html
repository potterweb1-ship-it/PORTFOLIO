BuffaLouie's

BuffaLouie’s, known as the “World’s Best Wings” spot in Bloomington, opened a new location in Carmel, Indiana. The opening needed a full print package ready for day one.
I designed promotional flyers, dine in and catering menus, plus build guide boards for the kitchen.
Role
Graphic Design
Scope
Promotional Material
Tools
Illustrator · Photoshop · InDesign
01Problem
The new Carmel location needed materials that could bring people in, help guests order, and give the kitchen clear directions from the first day.
02Solution
Every piece uses the same BuffaLouie’s look, including the cream, red, and blue color palette, hand lettered logo, and Grandpa Lou character. The goal was to make the restaurant feel consistent for customers in the dining room and staff working behind the scenes.
